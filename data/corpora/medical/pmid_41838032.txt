TITLE: Weight Regain Reverses Caloric Restriction-Induced Benefits on the Insulin-IGF-1 Nutrient-Sensing Pathway: Post Hoc Analysis From the CALERIE-2 Randomized Controlled Trial.

ABSTRACT:
To investigate the long-term metabolic and hormonal consequences of sustained weight loss versus weight regain after 1 year of caloric restriction (CR), with attention to insulin resistance and type 2 diabetes risk.
