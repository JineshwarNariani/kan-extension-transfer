TITLE: Empowering Women Through Movement: A Randomized Controlled Trial Comparing the Effects of Strengthening Exercises and Tai Chi on Menstrual Symptoms, Pain, and Psychological Outcomes in Primary Dysmenorrhea.

ABSTRACT:
Primary dysmenorrhea affects women's quality of life through menstrual pain and associated psychological distress. While pharmacological treatments are available, concerns over side effects highlight the need to explore non-pharmacological approaches. This study compared the short-term effects of strengthening exercises and Tai Chi on menstrual symptoms, pain, and self-reported stress, anxiety, and depression in women with primary dysmenorrhea.
