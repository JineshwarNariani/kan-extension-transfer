TITLE: Glycemic Status and Effect of Immediate Intensive Statin on Mild Ischemic Stroke: A Subgroup Analysis of the INSPIRES Trial.

ABSTRACT:
A subgroup analysis of the Intensive Statin and Antiplatelet Therapy for High-risk Intracranial or Extracranial Atherosclerosis (INSPIRES) trial to evaluate intensive statin effects in patients with ischemic stroke by glycemic status.
